Who?

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University. I am a Research Assistant affiliated with the Center for Language and Speech Processing and advised by Dr. Sanjeev Khudanpur and Dr. Philipp Koehn. My main research interests are speech recognition, machine translation, natural language processing and machine learning.
