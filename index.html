Who?

I am a second year M.S.E. student in the Department of Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University. I am a Research Assistant currently affiliated with the Center for Language and Speech Processing. My main research interests are speech recognition, machine translation, natural language processing and machine learning.

Research

Some of my current research projects are :

  • Speech Translation (with Dr. Sanjeev Khudanpur, Dr. Matt Post and Dr. Daniel Povey)
    • In recent times, I have focused on investigating techniques for translating conversational telephone Spanish and Arabic speech. Specifically, I look at how ASR and SMT systems can be combined to achieve an optimal translation output.
    • I built an end-to-end sandbox for speech translation using Kaldi and Joshua.
    • I helped build a dataset for Speech Translation research (coming soon here)
  • Machine Learning and Molecular Dynamics (with Dr. Tom Woolf and Dr. Yanif Ahmad)
    • Predicting the trajectory of protein molecules (EPAC-2)
    • Drug Design : Estimating the binding affinity of ligands and proteins (GCP-II).
    • Predicting cancer causing mutations in the PTEN protein using features from Homology Modelling
