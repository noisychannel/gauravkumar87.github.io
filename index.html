Who?

I am a second year M.S.E. student in the Department of Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University. I am current affiliated with the Center for Language and Speech Processing. My main research interests are speech recognition, machine translation, natural language processing and machine learning.

Research

Some of my current research projects are :

  • Speech Translation (with Dr. Sanjeev Khudanpur, Dr. Matt Post and Dr. Daniel Povey)
    • I focus on conversational telephone speech.
    • I recently built an end-to-end sandbox for speech translation using Kaldi and Joshua.
  • Machine Learning and Molecular Dynamics (with Dr. Tom Woolf and Dr. Yanif Ahmad)
    • Predicting the trajectory of protein molecules (EPAC-2)
    • Drug Design : Estimating the binding affinity of ligands and proteins (GCP-II).
    • Predicting cancer causing mutations in the PTEN protein using features from Homology Modelling
